Key Observations

  • CSS box-shadow spread on ring (border): spread outsets the BOX shape, not the ring shape. The shadow is a solid ellipse outset by spread, not a ring with adjusted radii.
  • Figma spread on ring: outer circle expands AND inner hole shrinks — this is a geometric outset on the shape (outer radius +spread, inner radius -spread), NOT an alpha-channel dilate. A pixel dilate would turn the circle into a rounded rectangle.
  • Figma spread on image fill: shadow becomes a solid rectangle — uses frame bounds, not content alpha.
  • CSS has no equivalent to Figma's ring spread behavior. box-shadow ignores border shape, drop-shadow has no spread.
